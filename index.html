Now, Not Yet

Released Aug 2019

Musicians:

  • Josh Taylor, Lead vocals & Guitar
  • Brett Kramer, Drums
  • J. Tyler Johnson, Bass Guitar

The album explores the tension behind dichotomies such as life and death, healing and suffering, good and evil–all of which are a part of the human experience–as well as the impact of faith and religion on a person’s well-being. half•alive shared in a 2019 interview that their goal for the album is: ”to speak into that tension and provide hope and a way into a life that is the fullest”.
